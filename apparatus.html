Apparatus Manual

Learn how to use laboratory apparatus safely

🔬 Microscope

Purpose: Used to observe microscopic specimens and cells.

How to Use: Place the slide on the stage, adjust the objective lens, and slowly focus using the coarse and fine knobs.

Safety: Clean lenses with lens paper only and switch off after use.

Optical Instrument

⚗️ Burette

Purpose: Used in titration to deliver accurate liquid volumes.

How to Use: Clamp vertically, fill carefully, remove air bubbles, and record readings at eye level.

Safety: Wash with distilled water after use.

Volumetric Apparatus

🧪 Pipette

Purpose: Transfers fixed liquid volumes accurately.

How to Use: Use a pipette filler, draw liquid slowly, and release carefully into the container.

Safety: Never pipette using mouth suction.

Measurement

🥼 Beaker

Purpose: Used for holding, mixing, and heating chemicals.

How to Use: Pour liquids carefully and place on a wire gauze while heating.

Safety: Do not use cracked beakers.

Container

🔥 Bunsen Burner

Purpose: Produces flame for heating experiments.

How to Use: Connect gas supply, ignite carefully, and adjust air hole for flame control.

Safety: Keep flammable materials away.

Heating Equipment

⚖️ Electronic Balance

Purpose: Measures mass with high precision.

How to Use: Switch on, press tare button, and place sample gently.

Safety: Avoid spilling chemicals on the balance surface.

Measurement Instrument

🌡️ Thermometer

Purpose: Measures temperature changes during experiments.

How to Use: Immerse bulb properly without touching container walls.

Safety: Handle carefully to prevent breakage.

Temperature Device

🧫 Petri Dish

Purpose: Used for culturing bacteria and microorganisms.

How to Use: Sterilize before use and keep covered during experiments.

Safety: Dispose biological samples properly.

Biology Equipment

🧲 Magnetic Stirrer

Purpose: Automatically mixes solutions using magnetic rotation.

How to Use: Place stir bar inside liquid and adjust speed gradually.

Safety: Keep electrical surface dry.

Mixing Equipment

💧 Wash Bottle

Purpose: Used for rinsing apparatus with distilled water.

How to Use: Gently squeeze bottle to direct water stream.

Safety: Label contents correctly before use.

Cleaning Equipment

🧬 Centrifuge

Purpose: Separates substances by rapid spinning.

How to Use: Balance tubes equally before starting machine.

Safety: Never open lid while rotating.

Separation Equipment

🧯 Fume Hood

Purpose: Removes harmful chemical fumes from workspace.

How to Use: Perform reactions inside hood with glass shield lowered.

Safety: Ensure ventilation is running before experiment.
