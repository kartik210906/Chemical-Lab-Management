⚠️ Laboratory Safety Guidelines

Follow proper safety procedures while performing experiments and handling chemicals.

🥼 Wear Proper Lab Equipment

Always wear lab coat, gloves, and safety goggles before entering the laboratory.

  • Wear closed shoes only
  • Keep long hair tied properly
  • Avoid loose clothing near flame

🧪 Chemical Handling

Read chemical labels carefully before use and follow storage instructions properly.

  • Never taste chemicals
  • Use fume hood for strong acids
  • Do not mix unknown chemicals

🔥 Fire Safety

Handle burners and heating equipment carefully to prevent accidents.

  • Keep flammable materials away
  • Turn off gas after experiment
  • Know fire extinguisher location

⚡ Electrical Safety

Ensure electrical instruments are dry and properly connected before operation.

  • Do not touch wires with wet hands
  • Switch off devices after use
  • Report damaged equipment immediately

🧫 Biological Safety

Follow sterilization procedures while handling biological samples.

  • Dispose samples properly
  • Wash hands after experiments
  • Keep culture dishes covered

🚨 Emergency Procedure

In case of accident, inform the lab supervisor immediately and follow emergency instructions.

  • Use eye wash station if required
  • Call emergency support quickly
  • Evacuate calmly during major hazards
